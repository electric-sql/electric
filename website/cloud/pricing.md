# Pricing

Electric Cloud pricing information.

## Current Status

Electric Cloud is currently free in public BETA. We'll be launching low-cost, usage-based pricing soon.

## Free Tier

Electric Cloud will always provide a generous free tier, so many apps will roll over with zero cost.

## Enterprise

If your plan is to use Electric in a larger app (more than 1,000 monthly active users) please reach out to make sure we can fully support you and to get a sense of what the future pricing will be like.

## Contact

Let us know if you have any questions. We'll be very happy to help. You can ask questions on [Discord](https://discord.electric-sql.com) or email us directly at [support@electric-sql.com](mailto:support@electric-sql.com).
